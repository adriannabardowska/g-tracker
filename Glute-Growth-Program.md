# Glute Growth & Upper Body Program

## Profile
- **Goal:** Grow and lift glutes with upper-glute emphasis while keeping quad volume controlled.
- **Frequency:** 5 days per week.
- **Structure:** 3 lower/glute days, 1 push day, 1 pull day.
- **Tracking app:** `index.html` in the `g-tracker` GitHub Pages app.

## Weekly Split
- **Monday:** Lower A, heavy glutes
- **Tuesday:** Push
- **Wednesday:** Lower B, glute isolation
- **Thursday:** Pull
- **Friday:** Lower C, heavy glutes
- **Rest days:** Saturday and Sunday, or move rest days as recovery requires.

## Monday - Lower A (Heavy Glutes)
1. **Barbell Hip Thrust**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 3 min
   - Cue: Drive through heels, tuck pelvis at the top, and pause for a hard glute squeeze.

2. **Barbell Glute Bridge**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 2 min
   - Cue: Keep ribs down, heels close enough to feel glutes, and hold the top for 2 seconds.

3. **Bulgarian Split Squat**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps per leg
   - Rest: 90 sec
   - Cue: Use a long stance, lean forward, keep the front shin controlled, and drive through the heel.

4. **Cable Straight-Leg Kickbacks**
   - 3 sets x 10-12 reps per leg
   - Rest: 90 sec
   - Cue: Keep the knee mostly straight and move from the hip without arching your lower back.

5. **Hip Abduction Machine**
   - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
   - Rest: 60 sec
   - Cue: Control the opening and closing; avoid using momentum.

## Tuesday - Push
1. **Incline Dumbbell Press**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 90 sec

2. **Arnold Dumbbell Press**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 90 sec

3. **Dumbbell Bench Press**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 90 sec

4. **Dumbbell Lateral Raise**
   - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
   - Rest: 60 sec

5. **Triceps Pushdown**
   - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
   - Rest: 60 sec

## Wednesday - Lower B (Glute Isolation)
1. **Romanian Deadlift**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 2 min
   - Cue: Hinge from the hips, keep a soft knee bend, and stop where you still control your back position.

2. **Barbell Hip Thrust**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 2 min
   - Cue: Use a controlled tempo and squeeze at the top without overextending your back.

3. **45-Degree Back Extensions**
   - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
   - Rest: 90 sec
   - Cue: Round upper back slightly, hinge at the hips, and finish by squeezing glutes.

4. **Cable Straight-Leg Kickbacks**
   - 3 sets x 10-12 reps per leg
   - Rest: 90 sec

5. **Hip Abduction Machine**
   - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
   - Rest: 60 sec

## Thursday - Pull
1. **Lat Pulldown**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 90 sec

2. **One-Arm Dumbbell Row**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps per side
   - Rest: 90 sec

3. **Seated Cable Row**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 90 sec

4. **Rear Delt Row or Face Pull**
   - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
   - Rest: 60 sec

5. **Barbell or Dumbbell Curl**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 60 sec

## Friday - Lower C (Heavy Glutes)
1. **Sumo Squat**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 2 min
   - Cue: Use a wide stance, sit back into the hips, and keep the movement glute-focused.

2. **Barbell Hip Thrust**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 3 min
   - Cue: Aim to match or beat Monday only if form stays clean.

3. **Barbell Glute Bridge**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 2 min

4. **Bulgarian Split Squat**
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps per leg
   - Rest: 90 sec

5. **Cable Straight-Leg Kickbacks**
   - 3 sets x 10-12 reps per leg
   - Rest: 90 sec

6. **Hip Abduction Machine**
   - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
   - Rest: 60 sec

## Progression
- Keep using the app's previous-weight suggestions from logged workouts.
- When all sets hit the top of the rep range with clean form, increase load next time.
- If a lower day feels too quad-dominant, reduce load and emphasize long stance, heel drive, and hip hinge.
- If recovery drops, cut one accessory set before cutting the main hip thrust work.

## Notes
- Single-leg hip thrusts and single-leg glute bridges are removed from the active plan.
- RDLs appear only on Wednesday.
- Frog pumps are removed from the active plan.
- Chest remains limited to Tuesday pressing work.
